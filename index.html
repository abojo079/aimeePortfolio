About Me

I was born and raised in the city of Miami, Florida. I attended North Miami Beach Senior High School and graduated in 1998. Soon after graduating from High School, I joined the United States Army and served a total of eight years. During my years of service I did several tours in both Iraq and Afghanistan. Upon my completion of service, I returned home to Miami, floida.

Soon after returning, I enrolled into Florida International University and graduated with a Bachelors Degree in Criminal Justice with a minor in International Relations in 2005. In 2012, I returned to Florida International University and graduated with a Masters Degree in Pubic Administration with a focus in Emergency Management.

Since graduating in 2012, I've worked for Miami-Dade Fire Rescue as an Emergency Management Coordinator.
